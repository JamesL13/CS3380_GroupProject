History

Tony's Pizza Palace in Columbia, Missouri, is a neighborhood pizza restaurant that has been serving fresh family favorites, Greek meals, and Italian classics for more than 50 years.

Active Members of the Community

Family-owned-and-operated Tony's Pizza Palace is one of the greatest places you will ever find, offering great cuisine and an unbeatable family atmosphere. Our staff treats everyone like a close family member.

Old-School Cooking

Bringing with them the recipes and the knowledge that would become the backbone of Tony's Pizza Palace, Tony and Asimina Veros came to America in the early '60s looking for a better life. They found it here, and their work and dedication paid off, as their reputation is well-rooted in the community.
